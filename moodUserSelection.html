User Sign-In & Sign Up

Mood is a peronal online mood journal. It allows your to keep track of your mood, activity levels,
and any affecting weather conditions.

To add a user, simply enter your desired ID below and hit "Add User". You may then sign in with that
user ID. User data is stored in your personal browser. User ID is enforce so that multiple users on
the same browser may use this app. There is no sesnsitive information stored and as such user IDs are
not protected. Because this app uses your browser's storage, you will not be able to sign in from a
different browser and access your journal from there. If you clear your cahced data you may also loose
data.
